Pasta al Pesto

Recipe description

Trofie al Pesto alla Genovese with Potatoes and Green Beans. This is a traditional Ligurian dish made with trofie pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 (17.6-ounce) package of linguine
  • 1 (7.17-ounce) jar of Niasca pesto
  • Extra virgin olive oil, to taste
  • Sea salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Bring 6 quarts of water to a boil, and add enough salt to make it a bit less salty than the sea (about 2-3 tablespoons).Meanwhile, place the pesto in a sauté pan or a shallow mixing dish, and set aside.

  2. Step 2

    3. Cook the pasta in the boiling water until almost al dente, and drain, reserving 1 cup of the pasta's cooking water.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add the pasta to the pesto in the sauté pan or shallow mixing dish but do not place overheat! Toss to combine, and add a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil. If needed, add a small amount of the pasta cooking water to thin the pesto.

  4. Step 4

    5. When the pasta is completely coated with the pesto, place it into a warmed serving dish, and serve.
